Abstract

With the advance of machine learning and computer graphics, more and more researchers start applying similar techniques in biology. As such, simulating biological ecosystem with these techniques is widely popular. In particular, a 3D environment simulation helps provide a custom-made landscape and we could simulate the natural selection in an ecosystem through generations. For this project, we are interested in simulating a real world ecosystem to observe the influences of natural selection on the adaptability and survival of species, and the effects of overpopulation and competition on the whole ecosystem environment.
